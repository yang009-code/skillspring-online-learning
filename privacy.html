Privacy Policy

SkillSpring stores the information that is needed for the project to work. This includes a user name, email address, orders, enrolled courses, lesson progress, reviews, and contact messages.

Passwords are saved as password hashes. The site does not ask for a real credit card number, bank password, government identification number, or PayPal password.

An administrator can view user accounts and can disable an account when needed. This is a classroom demonstration, so users should not enter private or sensitive information.
